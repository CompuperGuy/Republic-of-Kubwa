Welcome to The Republic of Kubwa

The Republic of Kubwa is a proud nation dedicated to the ideals of free speach and democracy.

History

Bir Tawil is a unique region between Egypt and Sudan that remains unclaimed due to a border discrepancy. Neither country claims this land, creating a rare opportunity to establish a new nation. We intend to claim Bir Tawil to create a country founded on liberty, free speech, democracy, and hospitality. Kubwa will offer a safe home for Sudanese, Israeli, and Ukrainian refugees seeking peace and a fresh start.

Our Vision

We are two people with a vision for a place that cherishes free speech and liberty. Kubwa will be a welcoming home for Sudanese, Israeli, and Ukrainian refugees, providing safety and opportunity for all.
